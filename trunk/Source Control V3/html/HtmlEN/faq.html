FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
  • How do you charge for your services?

    Take a look at our calculator or call us for a quote.

  • What Area do you cover?

    We cover all errands within Dubai & Sharjah, yet we can do pickups and drop offs in all seven emirates and Al Ain city as well.

  • Do I have to bring my parcel to you?

    No, we will collect it from you at the time of your convenience.

  • Is there a size or weight limit to my consignment?

    Please check our calculator page to get a quote for either a motorcycle or car errand.

  • What if a service I require is not listed?

    Just ask! We will do what we can to assist you. Requests for service which are illegal will not be granted.

  • How do I know my parcel will be safe?

    Your goods will not exchange hands, a dedicated messenger will pick up, transport and deliver your parcel personally to your desired destination.

  • Can you do same-day requests?

    Sure.

  • What are your working hours?

    SAT – THU 9:00 a.m. - 9:00 p.m. We are closed on Fridays.

  • Are you insured?

    Yes.

  • Can I change destination after collection?

    ure you can, just contact our call center and they will assist you in implementing all the changes you desire.
